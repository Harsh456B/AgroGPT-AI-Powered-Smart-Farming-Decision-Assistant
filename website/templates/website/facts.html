Amazing Agriculture Facts

Discover some fascinating facts about agriculture and farming in India and around the world.

India: 2nd Largest Producer
India is the world's second largest producer of fruits and vegetables, after China.
World's Largest Irrigated Area
India has the largest irrigated land area in the world, supporting diverse crops year-round.
Organic Farming Growth
India is among the top countries in organic farming, with millions of hectares under organic cultivation.
Monsoon Dependent
Over 60% of Indian agriculture is dependent on monsoon rains for irrigation.
Diverse Crop Basket
India grows more than 80 different crops annually, making it one of the most diverse agricultural nations.
Major Exporter
India is a major exporter of rice, spices, cotton, and tea, supplying to countries worldwide.
Tractor Revolution
The invention of the tractor in the early 20th century revolutionized farming, making large-scale agriculture possible worldwide.
Bees: Nature's Top Pollinators
One out of every three bites of food you eat exists because of pollinators like bees, making them vital to global agriculture.
